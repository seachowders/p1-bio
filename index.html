Hobbies as Escapism

The 'battle' for peace of mind has been waged for as long as humanity has existed. I think that in our digital age, with our brains are receiving and processing more information than ever, actively pursuing peace of mind has never been more important. I gravitate towards activities that are able to put me in a state in which I am fully engaged in the moment and time seems to disappear.
